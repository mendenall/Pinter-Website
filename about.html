80’S AND 90’S MAGIC.

The music hits, a chorus comes around, and the whole room starts singing. That’s the heart of Pinter Whitnick: familiar songs that pull people in and bring them together.

Their set moves from 80s anthems to moody 90s alt-rock, drawing from artists like Whitney Houston, Billy Idol, Radiohead, and The Cranberries. Kayleigh and Alex share vocals, layering guitar, bass, keys, and drums, while a live light show wraps the room in color.

By the time the last note fades, strangers feel like friends, and no one’s ready for the night to end.
